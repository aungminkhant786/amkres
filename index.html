About Me

I’ve always been interested in how computers work and how websites are built, which led me to pursue a degree in IT. I come from a supportive family who has always encouraged me to follow my dreams. Their belief in the importance of education has motivated me to work hard and strive for success. They’ve taught me to stay determined and never give up, which has been a big part of my journey. In the future, I’d like to explore artificial intelligence and its potential to solve real-world problems. My goal is to become a software developer and contribute to the tech industry with my skills.
